About This Site


The international dimension of Illinois has quickly become an integrated aspect of our campus. This website is intended to capture the multifaceted nature of internationalization at Illinois, and ease access to international-related information and resources across campus.

While the site is maintained by the Office of International Programs & Studies (IPS), its goal is to be as comprehensive as possible in presenting the range of international activities, assets, and linkages at Illinois.

Illinois Around the World adds a new dimension to this task by consolidating a wealth of international-related data with program information and upcoming events, and making it searchable by country, unit, or activity type. Units from across campus can add information to Illinois Around the World.

If you have suggestions about links, resources, or any other information that should be included in this website, please contact ips@illinois.edu.
