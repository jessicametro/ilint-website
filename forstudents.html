Study Abroad

Interested in being a part of the University of Illinois’ global vision?

Simply choose which fund below best describes the intent of your gift and click “Donate,” after which you will be directed to the University of Illinois Foundation’s secure giving page to complete your transaction.

IPS Annual Fund

Your gift supports a wide range of international activities both on-campus and around the world, from overseas faculty research and study abroad to development of new courses on vital global issues.

Study Abroad Scholarships Fund

Your gift enables Illinois students to participate in short- or long-term study abroad programs all over the world.

International Student Support Fund

Your gift provides support for Illinois’ international student population, several of whom are a long way from home.

Other Ways to Give

For other giving options (such as Electronic Funds Transfer and Payroll Deduction), to determine if your employer participates in a matching gift program, or to update existing account information, please visit the University of Illinois Foundation website.

(Note: When making a gift directly on the Foundation’s website, remember to indicate for which of the above three funds your gift is intended.)
